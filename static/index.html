How Satori Deciphers Handwritings

Traditional OCR models struggle with variable line weights, handwriting tilts, and character scale. Our CNN employs localized kernel convolutions that scan spatial sections of the canvas.

By normalizing user ink via centripetal center-of-mass alignment, Satori aligns the drawing center to training benchmarks, maximizing character similarity and prediction reliability.
